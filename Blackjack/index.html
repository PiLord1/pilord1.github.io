Blackjack Game Mechanics

Welcome to Blackjack, where every card could bring you victory or defeat!

Card Values:

  • Face Cards (Kings, Queens, Jacks): 10 points each.
  • Number Cards (2-10): Worth their face value.
  • Aces: Either 1 or 11 points—your choice.

Objective:

Get as close to 21 as possible without going over. A perfect 21 with your first two cards is a "Blackjack" and an automatic win!

How to Win:

  • Blackjack: Hit exactly 21 with your first two cards.
  • Beat the Dealer: End with a higher total than the dealer without exceeding 21.
  • Dealer Bust: If the dealer goes over 21, you win automatically.

Your Choices:

  • Hit: Draw one more card, but risk busting if you go over 21.
  • Stand: Keep your current total and see if it’s enough to win.

Take your shot at beating the dealer—will you play it safe or take the risk? Let the games begin!
